The Best movies according to Nandini

My favourite Animated movies

The Lion King

This film is about Simba, a lion cub, heir to the throne of "The Pride lands of Africa". He loses his father at a young age and the film portrays his journey of self-realisation as he overcomes his struggles and become the king he was always ment to be.

Kung Fu Panda

This is about Po, a panda, who is a great fan of Kung Fu warriors of his town. This film show how an ordinary being like Po , son of a local noodle maker, becomes the "The Dragon warrior", and the saviour of his town.

Ice Age

This movie is about a mammoth, a sloth and a tiger who find a human baby in the middle of the forest. This is a hilarious adventure of three animals, trying to keep the baby alive and entertained as they bring it to safety, back to the human town.
